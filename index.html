Raphaëll Maïwen

Game programmer

Raphaëll is a programmer in an indie studio by day and an experimental game maker by night. They make games with unusual technologies like PowerPoint, and conducts various workshops. Their work focuses on queerness, intimacy and Britney Spears.

Projects (Work in progress - Pictures, videos and links to come!)

Guitar Ego

Competitive rhythm game. Uses 2 keyboards as guitars. Currently developping it.


They Seem to Be Very Good Friends

Short puzzle game about heteronormativity. Made for an online exhibition by Galerie Galerie.


If U Seek Amy

Important Britney Spears fan game. An RPG about parasocial relationships, and performance. Made for an exhibition by Sporobole, Elektra and Centre Bang.


La crise du logement: le jeu

A Monopoly satire to laugh and cry about the housing crisis in Montreal.


Good Vibrations

A massaging software where participants have to massage each other using a controller's vibrations. Show prompts to ask questions and check consent.


2 Words 1 Keyboard

A chaotic 2-players game where both players have to type out a word, and physically wrestle in order to do so.
